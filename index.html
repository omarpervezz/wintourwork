Merchant & Direct Investing

We are interested in supporting the capital structure of corporate leaders in building stronger and increasingly dynamic market leading organizations.

We support leaders primarily through financing strategies for buy + build, operational improvements, and short-term liquidity needs. We seek to support leaders in executing creative mergers and add-on acquisitions that can open their organizations to new products & geographic territories, greater distribution, and enhanced supply chains.

We can participate within family or founder held organizations, going-private transactions (public-to-private), public or private divestitures, and private equity secondaries (next stage of ownership or new group).
